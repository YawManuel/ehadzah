Building IaaS services

Infrastructure as a Services (IaaS), are the core components that are managed by the Cloud provider. Examples are: the core network, storage, physical machine, hypervisor.

Building PaaS services

Platform as a Service (PaaS) are services provided by the cloud providers that make you free to concentrate on what matters, great service and customer experiences by leaving all underpinnig resources to the cloud provider.
