Apple starts their yearly events with the World-Wide Developers Conference (WWDC), the conference that brings together Apple, developers and users. But with this event, Apple mostly engages with the developers, where Apple introduces their various operating systems new features and other updates. Starting with the iOS 14 of the iPhone, there's now an app library that groups both apps and app folders as one; widgets library, widgets stack that groups widgets and widgets also on a home screen. Siri updates, like making siri send audio message. Updates in Apple map, updates also in carplay by making carplay to unlock and lock your car using your iPhone. With macOS, Apple introduces macOS Big Sur with new outlook of the windows. Messages updated with a search feature, creating memoji, celebrating special events; New Apple map where you can create your own guide, preview of the place you are going on the map. MacCatalyst, made for developers for giving them a head start when creating an app for the macOS, this year developers can self-optimize the apps to fully control the pixels and other appearance; Safari, the first browser to introduce privacy to browsers by introducing cookies blocking, customize taps, inbuilt translation, 50% faster than chrome.
